About NOX

Who We Are

NOX is a competitive esports organization focused on teamwork, content creation, and community. Our goal is to build a powerful team where players and creators grow together.

Competitive Goals

We compete in major tournaments and strive to dominate the competitive scene while supporting every player on our roster.

Content Team

Our content team produces highlights, montages, and streams that showcase the talent of our community.

Community Focus

NOX is more than a team — it's a family. Our community is the foundation of everything we build.
